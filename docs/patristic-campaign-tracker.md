## Post-Campaign 4 Data Quality Pass

### Validator improvements (validate_canonical_data.py)
Two new rules added:

1. **`validate_author_work_affirmation_redundancy` (ERROR)** — Fires when ALL evidence passages for a `person_affirms_proposition` claim trace back (passage → source → work) to works that already carry `work_affirms_proposition` for the same proposition. The person claim is entirely redundant; the work claim covers the author's position.

2. **`validate_evidence_role_semantics` Rule 2 (WARNING)** — Fires on any active claim whose sole evidence has `evidence_role` of `contextualizes` or `mentions` only (no `supports`). Such evidence does not directly substantiate the claim.
