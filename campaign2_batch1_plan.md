## New passages (16)
### Justin (src-justin-first-apology)
- psg-justin-apol-5-6 — Logos became man, Jesus Christ; worship Father, Son, Spirit
- psg-justin-apol-13 — Christians worship by prayer/thanksgiving; Jesus Son in second place
- psg-justin-apol-21 — Word first-offspring of God produced without sexual union
- psg-justin-apol-33 — Isaiah foretold virgin birth; Mary of David's race received Gabriel's annunciation
- psg-justin-apol-46 — Those who lived by Logos are Christians; Socrates, Heraclitus

### Aristides (src-aristides-apology)
- psg-aristides-apol-1-2 — God uncreated, eternal; Christians trace religion from Jesus who came from heaven and took flesh from a Hebrew virgin
- psg-aristides-apol-15 — Christian community practices: love, care for poor, fasting, prayer, chastity
- psg-aristides-apol-16 — Christians show mercy to all; love enemies; these people have found the truth

### Athenagoras Embassy (src-athenagoras-embassy)
- psg-athenagoras-emb-4-6 — God uncreated; all things made by the Logos from Him; one God who framed all by Logos, holds all by Spirit
- psg-athenagoras-emb-10 — Son of God is Logos/mind of Father; Father in Son and Son in Father; Spirit an effluence as light from fire
- psg-athenagoras-emb-31-33 — Christians pursue chastity; one wife or none; second marriage is adultery; aim to be with God
- psg-athenagoras-emb-37 — Christians pray for emperor; obey rulers; offer rational worship not animal sacrifice

### Athenagoras On Resurrection (src-athenagoras-resurrection)
- psg-athenagoras-res-1-3 — God who created what did not exist can restore what was dissolved
- psg-athenagoras-res-25 — Body must rise with soul for just judgment; same composite nature must stand before God

### Melito (src-melito-peri-pascha)
- psg-melito-peri-pascha-66-71 — Christ is the Passover lamb slain in Abel's place, Isaac's place, the lambs' place
- psg-melito-peri-pascha-96 — He who formed man from earth was born of a virgin; he who hung the earth was hung on a tree; he who raised the heavens rose from the dead
